# Face Swap Live - Model Setup Instructions

## Required Models
Download the models below and place them in this folder (models/).

### Face Swapper Model:

**HIGH QUALITY MODEL**:
https://github.com/Userfrom1995/FaceSwapLive/releases/download/v1.0.0/inswapper_128.onnx

### Face Enhancement Model (Optional but recommended):

**FACE ENHANCEMENT**:
https://github.com/Userfrom1995/FaceSwapLive/releases/download/v1.0.0/GFPGANv1.4.pth

## Auto-Download
If you don't download any models manually, the application will automatically download:
- inswapper_128.onnx (high quality face swapping)
- GFPGANv1.4.pth (face enhancement)

## Model Information
- **inswapper_128.onnx**: ~530MB, provides the best quality face swapping results
- **GFPGANv1.4.pth**: ~332MB, enhances face quality and reduces artifacts

## Hardware Requirements
- **Recommended**: 4GB+ VRAM for optimal performance
- **Minimum**: 2GB VRAM (may be slower)
- **CPU**: Works on CPU but significantly slower
